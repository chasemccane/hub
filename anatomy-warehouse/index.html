Your Exact Workflow

Quoting → HubSpot Deal, Step by Step

You asked specifically about this. Here's how it works end-to-end on Shopify Plus.

1

Customer requests a quote

Via email, phone, or a "Request a Quote" form on the storefront (available through apps like QuoteSnap, fully integrated with Shopify).

2

Your team creates a Draft Order

In the Shopify admin, create a draft order for that customer's company. Their price list, payment terms, and tax exemption apply automatically from their profile.

3

Flow pushes to HubSpot automatically

A Shopify Flow trigger fires the moment a draft order is created. It sends the contact info, product data, and deal value to HubSpot's API — creating a Deal in seconds. No manual entry.

4

Invoice sent — customer pays online

Send the customer a payment link directly from the draft order. When they pay, the Shopify order is created and HubSpot updates automatically.

HubSpot Integration

Two paths, both supported

Option 1 — Low code: Official HubSpot app from the Shopify App Store syncs customers, products, and orders in real time. Pair with Shopify Flow to push draft orders into HubSpot as Deals.


Option 2 — Full control: Use the Shopify DraftOrder API + HubSpot API for a fully custom integration. Your devs (or a Shopify Partner agency) can build exactly what you need.
