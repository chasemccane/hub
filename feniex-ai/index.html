Meet Sidekick — Your AI Business Co-Pilot

Sidekick is Shopify's conversational AI assistant, embedded directly in the merchant admin. It understands your store's data and can take action on your behalf.

  • → Ask business questions in plain English — "What were my top 10 B2B customers last quarter?" and get instant, accurate answers.
  • → Trigger actions through conversation — "Apply free shipping to all orders over $2,000 this week" — done in seconds.
  • → Get strategic guidance — Sidekick can suggest pricing strategies, identify at-risk accounts, and recommend catalog improvements.
  • → Proactive alerts — Sidekick surfaces issues before they become problems: inventory shortfalls, unusual order patterns, cart abandonment spikes.

The Philosophy

Why Shopify Is Betting Everything on AI

Shopify's CEO Tobi Lütke has been direct: AI is not a feature Shopify is adding — it is the foundation Shopify is rebuilding on. The question isn't whether commerce will be AI-powered. It's who gets there first.

Shopify's approach is to give every merchant — from a 10-person manufacturer to a $500M enterprise — access to capabilities that used to require a team of data scientists, designers, and marketers.

The result: merchants compete not by having more people, but by having better tools. AI is the great equalizer in commerce.
